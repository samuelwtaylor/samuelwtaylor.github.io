What my project was about and why i chose to do it

For my project, I chose to do photography and editing and I have worked out that I am not the best at taking photos so I can improve with that and I might do another photography project in the future.

What i did with for my first photo

The first photo I took and edited of me dunking a basketball and all I did to edit this photo was just changed the sharpness the exposure and also the contrast.

My second photo

Then for my second picture I did one that was pretty similar to the last one this was a picture of a basketball hoop and I just changed the exposure the sharpness and I made it a bit more orange.

My third and best photo

My third picture was definitely the hardest and more complex thing to edit this one I tried to show movement in my photo so I took a burst shot which is just like taking a series of photos one after another. After I took them I went and basically staked them up and erased part of the one on top of the picture I wanted to show and I repeated this until I got all five of the same person in the one picture I also darkened the background and made four of the figures more transparent so that you would focus on the figure closest to the hoop.
